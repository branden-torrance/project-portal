How can the Pokemon Team Builder app help?

This Pokemon app has been designed to help anyone desiring to become a PokeMaster. With the convenient team builder functionality, you can organize and build yourself the perfect team that you wish to use when playing any of the original pokemon games. This app includes the original 150 Pokemon and provides you with a PokeDex, which allows you to search the database of the original Pokemon and get details on their Pokemon ID, Health Power, Attack Power, Defense, and Element Types!

History of Pokemon Games

Pokemon was invented by two Japanese men, Satoshi Tajiri (developer) and Ken Sugimori (artist). The original versions of Pokemon (Red and Blue versions) were released in Japan in 1996, and they were called Pocket Monsters instead of Pokemon. The games were then released in the United States in 1998, under the name "Pokemon". Alongside the game releases, they also released the Pokemon card game, which took to the world by firestorm. Along with the video game and card game, they also created a television series following the protagonist of a young boy named Ash Ketchum.
